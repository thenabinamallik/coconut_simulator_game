Welcome to the Coconut Experience

This interactive game is inspired by real-life observations and documentary storytelling. It explores the world from an unusual perspective the life of a coconut.

From growing on a tree, falling to the ground, drifting in water, and eventually becoming part of human life every stage represents part of its journey.

Throughout the experience you will observe how a coconut moves and exists within its environment.
